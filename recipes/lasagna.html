Lasagna

Back to Index Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat, cheese, and tomato sauce. It's a hearty and comforting meal that is perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 egg
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375째F (190째C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef over medium heat. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, and half of the Parmesan cheese. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Spread a thin layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 3 lasagna noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, half of the ground beef, and a third of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers.
  7. Top with remaining noodles, marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake for 30-40 minutes or until bubbly and golden brown.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.

Notes

Lasagna can be made ahead of time and stored in the refrigerator for up to 2 days before baking. It can also be frozen for up to 3 months. To reheat, bake at 350째F (175째C) until heated through.

Serving Suggestions

Serve lasagna with a side salad and garlic bread for a complete meal. It pairs well with a glass of red wine.
